FAQs

Will an automatic sprinkler system use more water?

No, in fact an automatic sprinkler system may use less water! Our sprinklers have been specially designed to use up to 35% less water than our nearest competitors, and you'll never have to worry about leaving the sprinklers on again!

Will an automatic sprinkler system water as well as I do by hand?

Our sprinkler systems will do a more efficient job, because we tailor our sprinklers to your lawn's diverse watering needs. You'll also never have to worry about accidentally missing a spot while you're watering again!

We get 40 inches or more of rainfall every year. Do we really need a sprinkler system?

If it rained at your house every three days, an equal amount every time, then you would not need a sprinkler system. The problem with relying on rain for watering your lawn, is that rain does not work like that; rain usually comes in ebbs and flows, with rain coming for a few days in a row, and then a dry spell of no rain. Even a few days without water can negatively affect your lawn's appearance!

What are the components of an automatic sprinkler system?

An automatic sprinkler system comprises of: a controller, which functions as the brain of thee system; valves, which open and close to stop and start; and sprinklers, which distribute water to specific areas. This can be customized with rain shut off devices and drip irrigation zones.
